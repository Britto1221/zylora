# Domains

Zylora supports platform subdomains, custom domains, DNS verification state,
preferred domains, expiry dates, renewal status, and reminder history.

Standard supported `.com` management defaults to USD 19 per year. Premium and
unusually priced extensions require a custom quote. Registration must use the
client's legal ownership details.

Reminder jobs run at 60, 30, 15, and 7 days before expiry. After the final
reminder, non-renewal liability follows the signed service agreement.

Registrar purchasing remains a provider/manual operation; the application
contains a safe manual fallback and records verification and renewal state.
