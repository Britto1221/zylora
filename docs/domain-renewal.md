# Domain Renewal

Standard `.com` management price: $19/year when the domain falls within the supported cost threshold.

The domain must be registered in the client's legal name.

Reminders:

- 60 days
- 30 days
- 15 days
- 7 days

After the final reminder, non-renewal is the client's responsibility under the service agreement.
Domain payments are separate from WhatsApp credits.
